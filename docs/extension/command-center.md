# Your Command Center on X.com

## The Power of the postfun Extension

The postfun Chrome Extension transforms your X.com experience into a powerful trading platform.

## The Copilot Feature

The context-aware "Copilot" tab shows different information depending on where you are:
- On your Home timeline: Shows trending content and opportunities
- On a specific tweet: Shows trading information and options for that specific content

## One-Click Trading

The extension allows you to go from seeing a tweet to owning a piece of its market in seconds, without ever leaving X.com.

## Seamless Login

The extension provides secure, one-click login for the main `postfun.xyz` website, using your Nostr identity to authenticate you across both platforms.
